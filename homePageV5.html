Sanamluang is the popular Thai restaurant situated in Southern California. It offers Classic Thai dishes presented in a stylish, modern setting featuring scenes of Thailand at a decent price. It is popular for its authentic Thai dishes, including various noodle plates. The name,SanamLuang originated from a park name in Bangkok famous for a ceremony marking the start of the growing season- also for its kite flying posibilities. Currently, Sanamluang has two locations in Los Angeles
